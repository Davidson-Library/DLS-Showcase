Scalar

Scalar is a free publishing platform that helps people create essay- or book-length digital scholarship. Scalar projects are called “books,” but they don’t have to be text-heavy. They can also be media-rich: users are empowered to bring together images, video, audio, charts, and maps and juxtapose them with their own writing. Scalar “books” can be created by individuals or by a group working collaboratively.

Omeka

Omeka is a free publishing platform that allows individuals or groups to build digital collections and curate those collections into online exhibits. Creators add items to their Omeka projects by writing metadata-rich records and uploading accompanying media files. They can then organize those items into collections and add pages of contextual information to direct online visitors through their exhibits.
