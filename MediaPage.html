The Digital Learning & Scholarship team is here to help faculty and students tell stories with podcasts, videos, and other media-rich formats. We can help you checkout digital media equipment, reserve our on-campus podcast studio, and learn editing software such as Adobe products and Audacity.

Podcasts offer a conversational and dynamic way to tell stories and share ideas using Audio. Podcasts can reach broad audiences through platforms like Spotify, Apple, and Google, making them a powerful tool for communicating with non-experts in a casual, engaging style. The medium is gaining popularity, and as of 2023, 41% of Americans had reported listening to podcasts within the last month. Popular formats include investigative reporting, interviews, and commentary. Podcasts leverage both writing and performance integrating multimedia elements to create compelling content.
